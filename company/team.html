Company Overview

Leadership

David Hekimian - Founder and CEO

Launched TechAccelerator in 2012 with one goal in mind: to create a platform that speeds up and simplifies the enterprise sales cycle. Mr. Hekimian is former CTO of Trace3, a Value-Added Reseller (VAR) of Enterprise network architectures for Fortune 500 companies. Over the course of fifteen years, Mr. Hekimian provided advisory roles with a variety of companies -- startups to mature corporations -- to help them develop product vision, go-to-market strategies, and scalable business models to increase revenue.
