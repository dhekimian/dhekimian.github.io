Customers are seeking new and faster ways to integrate disruptive technologies with their existing infrastructure in order to remain competitive. They are also more demanding than ever. They want a simple way to get hands-on with a product whenever, wherever.

Built for the Enterprise

TechAccelerator was founded by David Hekimian, the former CTO of Trace3, a Southern California-based Value-Added Reseller (VAR). Over the course of fifteen years, David worked with a variety of companies -- startups to mature corporations -- to help them develop product vision, go-to-market strategies, and scalable business models to increase revenue.

He became increasingly frustrated with the common sales hurdle culprits:

  • Long demo and PoC periods
  • Time wasted on bad opportunities
  • Effort required to maintain a lab
  • Inconsistent messaging across channel and sales teams
  • Difficulty scaling sales activities
  • Inability to quickly gather feedback and iterate
  • High cost of shipping equipment
  • Poor return on traditional enablement efforts

In 2012, David started TechAccelerator with one goal in mind: to create a platform that speeds up and simplifies the enterprise sales cycle.

The TechAccelerator platform has evolved over the years to empower your ecosystem. We enable:
  • Manufacturers
  • Value Added Resellers (VARs)
  • Distributors
  • System Integrators
  • Independent Software Vendors (ISVs)
  • Enterprise IT Departments
TechAccelerator provides on-demand, hands-on lab environments that educate, engage and ultimately empower enterprise customers, direct sales teams and channel partners, so they can get from demo to PoC to PO faster.
