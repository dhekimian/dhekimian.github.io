Why TechAccelerator

One Enviornment for Success

TechAccelerator provides on-demand, hands-on lab environments that educate, engage and ultimately empower enterprise customers, direct sales teams and channel partners, so they can get from demo to PoC to PO faster. We focus on Technology Vendors whose products are sold to Enterprise customers through multiple channels - Direct, Value Added Resellers (VARs) and Systems Integrators (SIs). These companies tend to focus on traditional IT Infrastructure - Storage, Networking, Backup and Security. Sales processes in these cohorts face one looming challenge, demonstrating the value and purpose of their solution. Overcoming their customer's litmus of questions and viability concers is accomplished through onsite demos and often build outs of PoC's in the production envirornments. Sales Engineering time, shipping hardware, various consultants, lead to unknown customer aquisition costs and delayed deal closes.
