What makes TechAccelerator different?

Building Lab Environments is Time Consuming.

PaaS makes it easy to deploy your first apps, but lacks the customization, privacy and cost controls you need to keep your business there forever.

IaaS is too flexible, requiring a large up-front investment in research and development to build a solution for your team.

Container Orchestration is hot technology, but introduces serious complexity challenges.

TechAccelerator offers the best way forward.

TechAccelerator gives your Developers single-command deploys for your apps, and simple ways to tune them for any scale.

TechAccelerator gives your DevOps engineers a battle-tested private architecture that is easy to set up, operate and modify.

TechAccelerator uses container technology as a means for fast deploys and cost savings, not as an technological end.

TechAccelerator turns the best cloud services into a cohesive open-source platform.

The result is a simple and powerful platform with the best security, reliability, performance and cost you can find anywhere.
